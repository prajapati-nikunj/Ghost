# ADR (Architecture Decision Record) Template

## Title: [Short Descriptive Title]
**Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated
**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Deciders**: [Name 1, Name 2]

## Context
What is the problem? Why is it a problem? What are the constraints and assumptions?

## Decision
What is the proposed solution? How does it solve the problem?

## Alternatives Considered
- **Alternative A**: Description + Pros/Cons
- **Alternative B**: Description + Pros/Cons

## Consequences
- **Positive**: What benefits do we gain?
- **Negative**: What are the trade-offs or new complexities?
- **Risks**: What could go wrong?

## Compliance
How will we ensure this decision is followed? (e.g., Lint rules, manual review, testing).
