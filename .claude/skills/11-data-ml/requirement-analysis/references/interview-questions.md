# Interview Questions for Surface & Adjust

## Vision Questions
- What is the single most important outcome for this project?
- Who is the primary end-user / customer?
- What does success look like in 1 week vs 3 months?
- Is this a new product or an extension of an existing system?

## Constraints Questions
- What is the budget and timeline?
- Are there mandatory technologies or platforms?
- Any regulatory or compliance requirements (GDPR, HIPAA)?
- What are the team's strengths and current skill set?

## Technical Questions
- What's the expected user load (DAU, peak concurrent)?
- Do we need offline support?
- What third-party integrations are required?
- What's the authentication strategy (SSO, social login, email/password)?
- What data must be persisted vs derived vs cached?

## Risk Questions
- What would cause this project to fail?
- What's the biggest unknown or assumption?
- What's the worst-case scenario if we ship with a bug?
- Is there a fallback plan if a key dependency is unavailable?

## Closure Questions
- Is anything I've summarized incorrect?
- What am I missing that you haven't told me yet?
- Are you comfortable with the proposed approach?
